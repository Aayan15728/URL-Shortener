Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how SwiftLink ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website, swiftlink.github.io (the "Service"), which provides QR code generation and URL shortening.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-Provided Content: When you use SwiftLink for QR code generation or URL shortening, you provide the original data (e.g., URL, text). This data is processed to deliver the service.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information like browser type, access times, and pages visited, which helps us improve the Service.
  • No Personal Identifiable Information: SwiftLink does not collect personal identifiers (e.g., name, email, IP address) unless they are explicitly part of the content you input for QR codes or URL shortening.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service (generating QR codes, shortening URLs).
  • Enhance and expand our Service.
  • Analyze Service usage patterns.
  • Identify and resolve technical issues.

3. Data Storage and Security

SwiftLink is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not maintain a persistent backend server for storing shortened URLs or QR code content. Data processing is primarily client-side (in your browser) or temporary for the duration of a request. While we take reasonable security measures, no online transmission or storage is entirely secure.

4. Third-Party Links

Our Service may include links to external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit, as we have no control over their content or practices.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we advise you to review it periodically. Changes become effective when posted.
